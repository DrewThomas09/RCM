## 1. Purpose

Business Associate will perform certain services for Covered Entity
pursuant to the **[UNDERLYING_SERVICE_AGREEMENT]** dated
**[UNDERLYING_AGREEMENT_DATE]** (the "Services"). In connection with
the Services, Business Associate may create, receive, maintain, or
transmit Protected Health Information ("PHI") on behalf of Covered
Entity. This Agreement sets forth the parties' respective rights
and obligations with respect to such PHI and is intended to comply
with the HIPAA Rules (45 CFR Parts 160 and 164).

---

## 2. Definitions

Terms used but not otherwise defined in this Agreement have the
meanings set forth in the HIPAA Rules. "PHI" has the meaning given
at 45 CFR 160.103, limited to information created, received,
maintained, or transmitted by Business Associate from or on behalf
of Covered Entity.

---

## 3. Obligations of Business Associate

Business Associate agrees to:

**3.1 Use and Disclosure.** Not use or disclose PHI other than as
permitted or required by this Agreement or as required by law.

**3.2 Safeguards.** Use appropriate administrative, physical, and
technical safeguards to prevent use or disclosure of PHI other than
as provided for by this Agreement, including compliance with 45 CFR
§§ 164.308, 164.310, 164.312, and 164.316 with respect to
electronic PHI. An inventory of implemented safeguards is
maintained in `HIPAA_READINESS.md` and incorporated herein by
reference.

**3.3 Reporting.** Report to Covered Entity any use or disclosure
of PHI not provided for by this Agreement of which Business
Associate becomes aware, including any breach of unsecured PHI as
required at 45 CFR 164.410, within **[NOTIFICATION_WINDOW_DAYS]**
calendar days of discovery.

**3.4 Subcontractors.** Ensure that any subcontractor that creates,
receives, maintains, or transmits PHI on behalf of Business
Associate agrees in writing to the same restrictions, conditions,
and requirements that apply to Business Associate with respect to
such information.

**3.5 Access.** Make available PHI in a designated record set to
Covered Entity within **[ACCESS_WINDOW_DAYS]** calendar days of
Covered Entity's written request, as necessary to satisfy Covered
Entity's obligations under 45 CFR 164.524.

**3.6 Amendment.** Make any amendment(s) to PHI in a designated
record set as directed by Covered Entity, pursuant to 45 CFR
164.526.

**3.7 Accounting.** Maintain and make available to Covered Entity
the information required to provide an accounting of disclosures
pursuant to 45 CFR 164.528.

**3.8 Internal Practices.** Make its internal practices, books, and
records available to the Secretary for purposes of determining
compliance with the HIPAA Rules.

**3.9 Return or Destruction.** At termination of this Agreement,
return or destroy all PHI received from, or created, maintained, or
received on behalf of, Covered Entity, if feasible. If return or
destruction is not feasible, extend the protections of this
Agreement to the retained PHI and limit further uses and
disclosures to those purposes that make return or destruction
infeasible.

---

## 4. Permitted Uses and Disclosures by Business Associate

**4.1 Services.** Except as otherwise limited by this Agreement,
Business Associate may use or disclose PHI to perform the Services,
provided that such use or disclosure would not violate the HIPAA
Rules if done by Covered Entity.

**4.2 Management and Administration.** Business Associate may use
PHI for the proper management and administration of Business
Associate or to carry out its legal responsibilities.

**4.3 Aggregation (optional).** Business Associate may use PHI to
provide Data Aggregation services to Covered Entity as permitted by
42 CFR 164.504(e)(2)(i)(B). *Delete this section if Data Aggregation
is not part of the engagement.*

**4.4 De-Identification (optional).** Business Associate may
de-identify PHI in accordance with 45 CFR 164.514(a)-(c).
De-identified information is not PHI and is not subject to this
Agreement. *Delete if not applicable.*

---

## 5. Obligations of Covered Entity

**5.1 Notice of Privacy Practices.** Provide Business Associate
with its notice of privacy practices and any changes thereto.

**5.2 Restrictions.** Notify Business Associate of any restriction
on the use or disclosure of PHI to which Covered Entity has agreed
that may affect Business Associate's use or disclosure of PHI.

**5.3 Changes in Authorizations.** Notify Business Associate of any
changes in, or revocation of, any authorization by an individual
that may affect Business Associate's use or disclosure of PHI.

---

## 6. Term and Termination

**6.1 Term.** This Agreement is effective as of the date first
written above and remains in effect until the termination of the
**[UNDERLYING_SERVICE_AGREEMENT]** or this Agreement is otherwise
terminated as provided herein.

**6.2 Termination for Cause.** Upon Covered Entity's knowledge of a
material breach by Business Associate, Covered Entity will either:
(a) provide an opportunity for Business Associate to cure the
breach within **[CURE_PERIOD_DAYS]** calendar days or (b) immediately
terminate this Agreement if cure is not feasible.

**6.3 Effect of Termination.** Upon termination, Business Associate
will comply with Section 3.9 (Return or Destruction).

---

## 7. Miscellaneous

**7.1 Regulatory References.** A reference to a section of the
HIPAA Rules means the section as in effect or amended.

**7.2 Amendment.** The parties agree to take such action as is
necessary to amend this Agreement from time to time as is necessary
for compliance with the HIPAA Rules and any other applicable law.

**7.3 Survival.** The obligations of Business Associate under
Section 3.9 survive termination of this Agreement.

**7.4 Interpretation.** Any ambiguity in this Agreement will be
resolved in favour of a meaning that permits Covered Entity and
Business Associate to comply with the HIPAA Rules.
