# PE & Healthcare Abbreviations (glossary)

Quick expansions of the acronyms a PEdesk user runs into, so the Guide can
answer "what does X stand for / mean". General domain definitions; for PEdesk's
own computed figures see the metric cards.

## Returns & fund
- MOIC — Multiple on Invested Capital (total value / invested equity).
- IRR — Internal Rate of Return (annualized, time-weighted).
- TVPI / DPI / RVPI — Total Value / Distributions / Residual Value to Paid-In.
- GP / LP — General Partner (manager) / Limited Partner (investor).
- NAV — Net Asset Value (the carried value of holdings).
- AUM — Assets Under Management. Vintage — a fund's first-investment year.

## Deal & structure
- LBO — Leveraged Buyout. EV — Enterprise Value. EBITDA — Earnings Before
  Interest, Taxes, Depreciation & Amortization. EV/EBITDA — the headline
  valuation multiple.
- QoE — Quality of Earnings. NWC — Net Working Capital. LOI — Letter of Intent.
  IC — Investment Committee. SPA — Sale & Purchase Agreement.
- R&W / RWI — Representations & Warranties / R&W Insurance. MAC — Material
  Adverse Change. MIP — Management Incentive Plan.
- DSCR / FCCR — Debt Service / Fixed-Charge Coverage Ratio. HHI — Herfindahl-
  Hirschman Index (market concentration). CR3/CR5 — top-3/5 concentration ratio.

## Healthcare & reimbursement
- CMS — Centers for Medicare & Medicaid Services. MA — Medicare Advantage.
  FFS — Fee-for-Service. VBC — Value-Based Care. ACO — Accountable Care
  Organization. MSSP — Medicare Shared Savings Program. RAF — Risk Adjustment
  Factor. APM — Alternative Payment Model. MIPS — Merit-based Incentive Payment
  System.
- RCM — Revenue Cycle Management. AR — Accounts Receivable; days-in-AR / DSO —
  Days Sales Outstanding. DRG — Diagnosis-Related Group. CMI — Case Mix Index.
  wRVU — work Relative Value Unit. NPSR — Net Patient Service Revenue.
- CCN — CMS Certification Number (provider id). NPI — National Provider
  Identifier. HCRIS — Healthcare Cost Report Information System (Medicare cost
  reports). HCAHPS — the CMS patient-experience survey.
- SNF — Skilled Nursing Facility. IRF — Inpatient Rehab Facility. LTCH — Long-
  Term Care Hospital. ASC — Ambulatory Surgery Center. HH — Home Health.
- Stark / AKS — physician self-referral law / Anti-Kickback Statute. CON —
  Certificate of Need. 340B — the federal drug-discount program. NSA — No
  Surprises Act. HIPAA — health-data privacy law.

## Note
These are standard industry definitions. When a PEdesk figure uses one of these
(e.g. days-in-AR, EV/EBITDA), the corresponding metric card gives PEdesk's
specific formula and caveats.
