# PEdesk Industry Intelligence (licensed IBISWorld-derived)

**Source:** Licensed IBISWorld US industry reports (purchased business license),
extracted into PEdesk-native structured data. **Raw PDFs are private/internal —
not committed, not served.** Only derived, attributed facts are stored.
**Powers:** `/industry`, `/industry/<slug>`, Research, diligence context panels,
and PEdesk-generated industry briefs.

**Coverage (5 reports):**
- Healthcare & Social Assistance (NAICS 62) — broad sector
- Primary Care Doctors (621111) — revenue ~$370.8bn, margin 11.5%
- Specialist Doctors (621112)
- Emergency & Other Outpatient Care Centers (62149)
- Hospitals (622110)

Each carries: at-a-glance metrics (revenue, employees, businesses, profit,
margin, wages), products/services mix, key external drivers, cost-structure
benchmarks (industry vs sector), industry definition + NAICS codes, and
PEdesk-authored diligence questions.

**What it can support:** industry-level market sizing, growth/margin context,
segment mix, demand drivers, cost-structure benchmarking, and framing a deal's
sector thesis — then pointing to the public datasets that can validate it.

**What it cannot support:** it is **not provider-specific** unless joined with
CMS/HCRIS/user data; **forecasts are report-derived, not PEdesk predictions**;
it is industry context, **not a final investment conclusion**. Report-derived
facts must never be presented as CMS/provider facts.

**PEdesk value-add:** every industry page links to real PEdesk surfaces that
validate or challenge the report — Hospitals → HCRIS X-Ray / cost structure /
payer stress; Primary Care → Physician Productivity (MIPS+HRSA) / CMS APM;
Specialist → Clinical Outcomes (CMS quality-measure); Outpatient → Dialysis /
Home Health / FDA drug-shortage supply risk.

**Attribution:** "Derived from licensed IBISWorld industry report, *<title>*,
*<publication date>*."
